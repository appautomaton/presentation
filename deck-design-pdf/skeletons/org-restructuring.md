# Organizational Restructuring — Skeleton

## Governing Thought

**Affirmative:**
```
"[Company] should restructure from [current model] to [target model],
 reducing [layers/spans/cost] by [X] while preserving [critical capabilities],
 achieving $[Y]M in annual savings and [Z]% improvement in decision speed."
```

**Negative:**
```
"[Company]'s performance decline is not an organizational problem — [root cause]
 is the binding constraint. Restructuring would destroy [critical capability]
 without addressing the underlying issue."
```

**CRITICAL — Capability Preservation Test:** Every restructuring must answer: "What visibility, capability, or institutional knowledge is LOST in the new model?" Before approving any target model, test what breaks.

## Pillar Architecture

| # | Pillar | Sub-Question | Transition Out |
|---|--------|-------------|----------------|
| 1 | **Diagnostic** | What's broken and why? | Synthesis → "multiple signals converge" |
| 2 | **Target Model Design** | What should the org look like? | Decision Gate → "choose option A, B, or C" |
| 3 | **Transition Planning** | How do we get from here to there? | Escalation → "design is right, execution risk is people" |
| 4 | **People & Change** | How do we bring people along? | Closes to recommendation |

## Kill Conditions

| # | Trigger | Pivot To |
|---|---------|---------|
| KC-1 | Performance decline caused by market/product, not org structure | Strategy or product intervention |
| KC-2 | Capability preservation test fails — critical knowledge destroyed | Hybrid model preserving critical teams |
| KC-3 | Key talent attrition >20% during restructuring | Retention-first program before structural change |
| KC-4 | Union/works council blocks required changes | Negotiated restructuring with extended timeline |
| KC-5 | CEO using restructuring to remove political opponents (not performance-driven) | De-scope to performance management only |

## Quality Gates

- [ ] Diagnostic uses ≥3 independent signals (not just cost benchmarking)
- [ ] Every design option tested against capability preservation matrix
- [ ] Spans-and-layers benchmarked against ≥5 comparable organizations
- [ ] RAPID maps show before AND after for ≥5 key decisions
- [ ] Transition cost fully loaded (severance, reskilling, productivity dip, systems)
- [ ] Communication cascade tested with pilot group before full rollout
- [ ] Stakeholder map covers all high-influence individuals with named engagement actions
- [ ] Change timeline benchmarked against comparable restructurings
