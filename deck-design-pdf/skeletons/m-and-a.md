# M&A / Due Diligence — Skeleton

## Governing Thought

**Affirmative:**
```
"[Company] should acquire [Target] at $[X]M ([Y]x [EBITDA/revenue])
 because [deal thesis in one clause], yielding [return: IRR/NPV]
 with [risk characterization] integration risk focused on [area]."
```

**Negative (Walk Away):**
```
"[Company] should not acquire [Target] at [price] because [dealbreaker].
 [Alternative] offers better risk-adjusted returns."
```

**Conditional (Bain-style):**
```
"[Company] should acquire [Target] IF [C1], [C2], [C3] hold.
 Evidence supports [C1] and [C2]; [C3] requires [specific DD action]."
```

## Pillar Architecture

| # | Pillar | Sub-Question | Transition Out |
|---|--------|-------------|----------------|
| 1 | **Deal Thesis** | Why this target, why now, why us? | Narrowing → "alternatives don't work" |
| 2 | **Commercial DD** | Is the revenue real, sustainable, growing? | Synthesis → "multiple lenses converge" |
| 3 | **Synergy Quantification** | What value does the combination create? | Escalation → "synergies real but realization requires X" |
| 4 | **Valuation & Structuring** | What should we pay and how? | Decision Gate → "math says [range]" |
| 5 | **Integration Planning** | How do we capture the value? | Closes to recommendation |

## Kill Conditions

| # | Trigger | Pivot To |
|---|---------|---------|
| KC-1 | >30% revenue in expiring contracts or >40% from top-3 customers | Walk away |
| KC-2 | Net synergies < acquisition premium | Renegotiate price or walk away |
| KC-3 | Integration >24 months and >$XM — incompatible culture/tech/regulatory | Partnership or minority stake |
| KC-4 | Cyclical peak valuation or regulatory timeline exceeds window | Wait and monitor with triggers |
| KC-5 | Competitive bidding pushes price above valuation range | Disciplined walk away |

## Quality Gates

- [ ] Deal thesis stands independent of synergy projections
- [ ] Revenue tested on ≥3 dimensions (concentration, contract mix, growth source)
- [ ] Synergies decomposed into specific mechanisms with owners and timelines
- [ ] Realization haircut applied (60-80% cost, 40-60% revenue) — deal still positive
- [ ] ≥3 valuation methods used, converge within 30% spread
- [ ] 100-day integration plan has specific actions with assigned owners
- [ ] Every kill condition explicitly evaluated and documented
- [ ] Helicopter test: titles of slides 2-22 tell complete deal story in 3 minutes
